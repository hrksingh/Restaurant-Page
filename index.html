North Sentinel Island is illegal to visit due to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands Protection of Aboriginal Tribes Regulation 1956, which prohibits travel to the island and any approach closer than five nautical miles to protect the Sentinelese tribe, a vulnerable indigenous group, from disease and exploitation. The Sentinelese have been known to violently resist any attempts at contact or approach by outsiders, adding to the island's reputation as a dangerous and forbidden place, but we won't kill you ☠️.
